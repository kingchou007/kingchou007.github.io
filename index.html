Jinzhou Li

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Robotics at Duke University, advised by Prof. Xianyi Cheng. I did my master's at Cornell University, where I was advised by Prof. Maha Haji and Prof. Daniel Hastings, and received my bachelor's degree from the University of Vermont. I also had the opportunity to work with Prof. Hao Dong at Peking University. My research interests include dexterous manipulation, tactile sensing, and reinforcement learning, aiming to enable robots to think and act more like humans.
