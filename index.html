Jinzhou Li

I am a PhD student in Robotics at Duke University, advised by Prof. Xianyi Cheng.


Previously, I worked with Prof. Hao Dong at Peking University and spent time as a research intern at AgiBot. I also had the opportunity to work with Prof. Maha Haji at Cornell and Prof. Daniel Hastings at MIT.


My research interests include dexterous manipulation, representation learning, and reinforcement learning to enable robots to act and think like humans.
